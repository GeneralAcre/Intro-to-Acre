First Story !

The longest survivor of the Titanic disaster was Millvina Dean. She was just two months old when the Titanic sank on April 15, 1912. She, along with her mother and brother, survived the sinking after being placed in lifeboat 10. Sadly, her father perished in the disaster. Millvina Dean lived until May 31, 2009, passing away at the age of 97. She was the last living survivor of the Titanic.
